
LAN:- Tend to use specific connectivity technologies, like ethernet and token.	
WAN:- WANs uses technologies like Frame Relay and X.25 for connectivity for the longer distances

LAN:- LAN is a computer network that covers a small geographic area, like a home, office, or group of buildings.	
WAN:- WAN is a computer network that covers a broad area. For example, any network whose communications links cross-regional and metropolitan boundaries over a long distance.

LAN:- There is a need to set up a couple of extra devices on the network, so it is not very expensive.	
WAN:- In WAN networks as remote areas have to be connected, hence the set-up costs are higher.

LAN:- LAN has a higher data transfer rate.	
WAN:- WAN has a lower data transfer rate as compares to LAN.

LAN:- Ownership of LAN is private.	
WAN:- WAN’s ownership can be private or public.

LAN:- The speed of LAN is high.	
WAN:- The speed of WAN is slower than LAN.

LAN:- The propagation delay time is short in LAN.	
WAN:- The propagation delay in WAN is long(longer than LAN).

LAN:- It offers more fault tolerance.	
WAN:- It offers less fault tolerance in WAN.

LAN:- Its design and maintenance is easy.	
WAN:- Its design and maintenance is difficult.
