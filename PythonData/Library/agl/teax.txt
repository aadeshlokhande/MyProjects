1 VER 2 VOL 3 DATE 4 TIME 5 CLS .
1) VER : 
This command is used to display the window's version.

Process:
step 1: Click on stoot.
step 2: click on all program.
step 3: Select Assessories 
step 4: Select command prompt.
step 5: Type "VER"
step 6: Exit.


2) VOL :
This command is used to display the disk volume level or serial number.

Process:
step 1: Click on stoot.
step 2: click on all program.
step 3: Select Assessories 
step 4: Select command prompt.
step 5: Type "VOL"
step 6: Exit.
